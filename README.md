# Women in AI – WomenThinkCode Event

Empowering conversations, responsible innovation, and practical AI insights—led by women, for women in tech.

---

## 🌟 Event Overview

The **Women in AI** event was hosted by **WomenThinkCode**, an initiative under **WeThinkCode_**, creating a space for women to explore the transformative role of Artificial Intelligence in today’s technology landscape. The event focused on **AI awareness, responsible AI, and practical applications**, inspiring attendees to actively engage with AI as creators, not just consumers. [1](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/dikeledi-kwenaite-145327346_womeninai-womenthinkcode-ai-activity-7371538695159533568-9qR4)[2](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/mary-mdukaza_womeninai-wtc-womenthinkcode-activity-7341149528424599553-JX8v)

---

## 🎯 Purpose of the Event

- Create an inclusive platform for women to learn and engage with AI  
- Spark conversations around **Responsible AI** and ethical considerations  
- Showcase how AI tools can be used practically in real‑world scenarios  
- Celebrate women actively shaping the future of AI and technology  

[1](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/dikeledi-kwenaite-145327346_womeninai-womenthinkcode-ai-activity-7371538695159533568-9qR4)

## 🎤 Speakers

### 👩‍💼 Carike Botha  
Development Consultant & Microsoft AI / Power Platform Specialist  
- Spoke about the **practical use of AI** in modern workflows  
- Demonstrated how tools like **Microsoft Copilot**, **Power Platform**, and AI‑assisted automation can empower users and teams  
- Emphasised that AI can act as both a **defender and an enabler** when used responsibly
